The McLaren GT is newer, furutristic model of McLaren cars. it has a 4.0 L twin-turbocharged V8 engine. The new engine, owever, delivers a lower output, because it has smaller turbochargers. The McLaren GT has an average output of 612 hp with a maximum torque of 465 lb-ft at a rate of 5500 rpm.

Suspension

The McLaren GT has a suspension system. The car takes the advanage of double wishbones at the front and back axles. It also has an active damping system which is called Proactive Damping system. The Proactive Damping System keeps tires in contact with the gravel, or dirt, or sand at all times. this system prevents tires from losing contact from the ground. If these tires loose contact with the ground at great speeds, it would compromise the full drive of the car, and there will b an accident.

Performance

The McLaren GT as a top speed of 203 mph, and it can accelerate from 0 - 60 mph in 3.1 seconds and 0 - 124 mph in 9 seconds. It takes a few more seconds compared to other super cars because it is heavy and built for luxury.
